# Scored Keyword Tables - Strataline.ca

**Methodology:**
- Volume estimates based on cross-referencing keysearch.co, rankvista.com, techwebers.com global data, adjusted to Canadian market (8-12% of US) and Toronto/GTA share (~40-50% of Canadian)
- Volume column = estimated Canadian monthly searches

- Relevance: how well keyword matches Strataline's actual service offering
