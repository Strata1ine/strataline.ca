## Site-wide patterns

### Strengths
- Popcorn removal page is the strongest SEO page: location keywords in alt text, primary keyword in title/desc/seo, good density
- Stairs page has heavy keyword saturation in alt text (intentional SEO strategy with location-based filenames)
- Painting page targets luxury/high-end niche well with geographic terms in seo field
- business.json structured data is thorough with areaServed and keywords arrays

### Weaknesses
- Homepage title "Dust-Free Renovation" is too short, missing "Toronto" or "GTA" geo-modifier
- Homepage meta desc lacks geo-targeting entirely
- Doors & windows seo field is a raw keyword dump, not a sentence - will render poorly as meta description
- Energy-efficient upgrades seo field is also a comma-separated keyword list, not prose
- Bathrooms, kitchens, garage doors, wallpaper pages have ZERO geo-keywords in title, desc, or seo fields
- No page uses "general contractor" or "renovation contractor" as a keyword despite business.json @type being GeneralContractor
- Alt text on bathrooms page is excessively long (30-40 words each, should be 5-12)
- Cover image alt text on garage doors is 30+ words - way too long
- Kitchens cover alt is 25+ words
- Stairs desc field is just a keyword list, not a compelling description

### Content length issues
- Energy-efficient upgrades page is significantly longer than other service pages - 10 FAQ answers may be excessive
- Stairs FAQ answer #3 is 40+ words of filler about "competitive prices and timelines that work with your budgets"
- Homepage hero desc at 23 words is appropriate
- TextCarousel items are appropriately punchy across all pages

### Missing keyword opportunities
- "dust-free" differentiator is underused on non-popcorn service pages (only stairs mentions it in one ZigZag)
- "Vaughan" (company HQ location) appears nowhere in page-level SEO fields except energy-efficient-upgrades
- "Toronto renovation" / "GTA contractor" never appears as a phrase
- No page targets "near me" intent or includes neighborhood-level terms
