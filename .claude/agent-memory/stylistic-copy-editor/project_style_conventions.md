## Sentence case
All titles, headings, benefit titles, zigzag titles, FAQ titles, section titles, and TextCarousel items must use sentence case. Only first word + proper nouns capitalized.

## Proper nouns to keep capitalized
- Strataline, Toronto, Vaughan, Mississauga, Richmond Hill, Markham, GTA, Burlington, Newmarket, Aurora, North York, Forest Hill, Oakville, Pickering, Ajax, Whitby, Oshawa, Brampton
- CityTV, CityLine, HGTV, The Star, House & Home, Scott McGillivray
- Benjamin Moore, Aura, Regal Select, Sherwin Williams, Emerald, Farrow & Ball
- Festool Planex, HEPA, ENERGY STAR, HVAC, HRV/ERV, LED

## Dashes
Never use em dash (—) or double dash (--). Always use single hyphen-minus (-) with spaces: ` - `.

## Description length targets
- Service page `desc` field: 1-2 sentences, 15-30 words
- Benefit card `desc`: 1-2 sentences, 15-25 words
- ZigZag item `desc`: 1-3 sentences, 15-40 words (some like air sealing are longer for SEO)
- FAQ `desc`: 1-3 sentences
