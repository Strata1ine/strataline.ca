<Card.Faq
  title="How long does the process take?"
>

A kitchen renovation can often be completed in just 4-8 weeks, depending on the scope. With proper planning, timely ordering of materials, and an organized approach, even larger projects can move smoothly. Minor updates like new countertops or a fresh backsplash may take just a few weeks, while more extensive remodels with new cabinets and appliances might take a couple of months. Good preparation and clear communication can ensure your dream kitchen comes to life quickly and efficiently!

</Card.Faq>

<Card.Faq
  title="What are the most important upgrades to prioritize?"
>

When renovating a kitchen, focus on upgrades that enhance functionality, style, and convenience. Start with high-quality cabinets with smart storage solutions and durable countertops like quartz or granite. Upgrade to energy-efficient appliances, and improve the space with layered lighting, including task, ambient, and under-cabinet options. Add a stylish backsplash for protection and flair, and complete the look with a modern sink, a high-quality faucet with a pull-down sprayer, a hot water dispenser, and a water filtration system. Including a garbage disposal adds practicality for easier cleanup. These thoughtful upgrades create a beautiful and highly functional kitchen you'll enjoy for years..

</Card.Faq>

<Card.Faq
  title="What is the best countertop material?"
>

The best countertop material depends on your needs. Quartz is a top choice for durability, low maintenance, and style. Granite offers natural beauty and heat resistance but needs sealing. Marble is elegant but prone to staining. Butcher block adds warmth but requires upkeep. For a modern look, consider stainless steel or concrete. Quartz is the best all-around option for most kitchens.

</Card.Faq>
