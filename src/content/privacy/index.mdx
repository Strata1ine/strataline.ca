# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 06/Jul/2025

This Privacy Policy is intended for users in Canada.

### Information Collection

We use Netlify to process and collect your information. We collect the following data:

- Email address
- Phone number
- General location
- An optional photo

_The collected information is used solely for processing your service request._

### Data Deletion

Once your service request is complete, your personal data will be manually deleted by us about one year after the completion of your service. Please note that we sometimes delete your data sooner. If you would like your data removed at any time, please contact us using the information provided below.

### Consent

By submitting your information, you consent to the collection, use, and storage of your data as described in this Privacy Policy.
