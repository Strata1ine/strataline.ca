_*This policy applies to users in Canada. If outside of Canada, this service is not applicable._

### Information collection

We use Netlify to collect and process your information, which may include:

- Email address
- Phone number
- General location
- Optional photo

_This is only used to handle your service request._

##### Reviews

You’re welcome to share reviews, which may include personal opinions or details. By submitting a review, you agree to its public display and our use. Reviews are moderated and treated as user-generated content, not personal data. We may edit or remove reviews if needed, including upon request.

##### Data deletion

We’ll delete your personal data about one year after your service is completed, though it may be sooner. If you’d like your data removed earlier, let us know using the contact info below.

##### Consent

By sharing your information, you agree to its collection, use, and storage as described here.

##### Policy updates

We may update this policy from time to time. If we do, we’ll update the "Last updated" date above. Feel free to check back anytime to stay informed.

##### Analytics

We use Ahrefs and Netlify for website analytics to understand how our site is performing, so we can reach more people.
